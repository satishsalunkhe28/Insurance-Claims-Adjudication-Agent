ZERO DEPRECIATION ENDORSEMENT

1. PURPOSE

The Zero Depreciation endorsement reduces or removes depreciation deductions on eligible replaced vehicle parts.

2. COVERAGE

Subject to policy terms, the endorsement may cover depreciation on:

- Plastic parts
- Rubber parts
- Fibre parts
- Metal parts
- Eligible replaced components

3. CONDITIONS

Coverage applies only when:

- The endorsement is active.
- The claim is otherwise admissible under the policy.
- The damaged parts require replacement due to a covered event.

4. EXCLUSIONS

The endorsement does not cover:

- Normal wear and tear
- Consumable items unless separately covered
- Mechanical breakdown
- Damage unrelated to an insured event
