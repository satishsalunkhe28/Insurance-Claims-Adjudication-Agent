ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE ENDORSEMENT

1. PURPOSE

Roadside Assistance provides emergency support when the insured vehicle becomes immobilized.

2. SERVICES

The service may include:

- Towing assistance
- Battery jump-start
- Flat tyre assistance
- Emergency fuel delivery
- Minor roadside repair
- Vehicle recovery support

3. LIMITATION

Roadside Assistance is a service benefit.

It does not automatically provide coverage for:

- Major vehicle repairs
- Engine replacement
- Mechanical breakdown costs
- Accident repair costs
- Wear and tear
