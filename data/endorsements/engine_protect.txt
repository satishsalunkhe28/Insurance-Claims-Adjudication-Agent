ENGINE PROTECT ENDORSEMENT

1. PURPOSE

The Engine Protect endorsement provides additional coverage for certain engine and gearbox damages that are not normally covered under the basic policy.

2. COVERED EVENTS

The endorsement may cover:

- Engine damage caused by water ingress
- Hydrostatic lock caused by flood water
- Damage to internal engine parts after accidental water entry
- Leakage of lubricating oil caused by accidental external damage

3. CONDITIONS

Coverage applies only when:

- The Engine Protect endorsement is active.
- Water entry occurred accidentally.
- The driver did not intentionally restart or repeatedly crank the engine after water entry.
- The damage is confirmed through inspection or repair assessment.

4. EXCLUSIONS

The endorsement does not cover:

- Normal wear and tear
- Mechanical breakdown unrelated to an insured event
- Pre-existing engine damage
- Intentional engine restart after known water ingress
- Damage caused by poor maintenance
- Damage caused by unauthorized modifications
