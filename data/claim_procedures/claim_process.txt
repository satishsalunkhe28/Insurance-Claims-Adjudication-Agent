MOTOR CLAIM PROCESS

1. CLAIM NOTIFICATION

The insured should notify the insurer as soon as reasonably possible after the incident.

2. CLAIM REGISTRATION

The claim is registered using:

- Policy number
- Claimant details
- Incident date
- Incident location
- Incident description
- Estimated claim amount

3. DOCUMENT COLLECTION

Required documents may include:

- Claim form
- Registration certificate
- Driving licence
- Policy copy
- Photographs
- Repair estimate
- Police report
- Surveyor report

4. COVERAGE REVIEW

The claim is checked against:

- Policy coverage
- Active endorsements
- Exclusions
- Policy validity
- Claim amount
- Supporting evidence

5. DECISION

The claim may be categorized as:

- Approved
- Claim Not Covered
- Manual Review Required

6. MANUAL REVIEW CONDITIONS

Manual review is required when:

- Required documents are missing.
- The incident description is insufficient.
- The claim amount needs surveyor verification.
- Fraud indicators are present.
- Policy clauses conflict.
- The agent has low confidence.
