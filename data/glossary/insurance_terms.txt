INSURANCE GLOSSARY

POLICY

A contract between the insurer and the insured.

INSURED

The person or vehicle protected by the insurance policy.

PREMIUM

The amount paid for insurance coverage.

ENDORSEMENT

An additional benefit or modification attached to the base policy.

EXCLUSION

A circumstance or event that the policy does not cover.

DEDUCTIBLE

The amount the insured must pay before the insurer contributes.

COVERAGE LIMIT

The maximum amount payable under the policy.

CLAIM

A request made to the insurer for payment or support after a loss.

CLAIM ADJUDICATION

The process of checking a claim against policy terms and deciding whether it should be approved, denied, or reviewed manually.

MANUAL REVIEW

A review performed by a human claims officer when the automated system cannot make a reliable decision.
