CLAIM HANDLING GUIDELINES

Insurance claims should be handled fairly, transparently, and within the applicable regulatory and policy framework.

The insurer should:

- Communicate claim requirements clearly.
- Request only relevant documents.
- Avoid unreasonable delay.
- Give a clear reason for claim decisions.
- Protect customer information.
- Maintain proper claim records.
- Refer complex or uncertain claims to an authorized human claims officer.
- Avoid rejecting a claim without sufficient policy evidence.
