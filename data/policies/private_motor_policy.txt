PRIVATE MOTOR INSURANCE POLICY

1. POLICY PURPOSE

This policy provides coverage for accidental loss or physical damage to the insured private motor vehicle, subject to the terms, conditions, limits, endorsements, and exclusions stated in the policy.

2. BASIC COVERAGE

The policy covers accidental physical damage to the insured vehicle caused by:

- Road accident or collision
- Fire
- Explosion
- Lightning
- Flood
- Cyclone
- Storm
- Earthquake
- Riot
- Strike
- Malicious external damage
- Theft or attempted theft

3. POLICY CONDITIONS

Coverage applies only when:

- The policy is active on the incident date.
- The vehicle is being used for private and permitted purposes.
- The driver has a valid driving licence.
- The incident is reported within a reasonable period.
- The insured provides truthful and complete information.
- Required documents are submitted.

4. REQUIRED CLAIM DOCUMENTS

The insurer may require:

- Completed claim form
- Vehicle registration certificate
- Valid driving licence
- Policy copy
- Incident photographs
- Repair estimate
- Police report, where applicable
- Surveyor report, where applicable

5. CLAIM ASSESSMENT

Every claim must be assessed against:

- Basic policy coverage
- Active endorsements
- Policy exclusions
- Claim documentation
- Policy limits
- Deductible amount

6. CLAIM SETTLEMENT

A covered claim may be approved subject to:

- Policy coverage
- Deductibles
- Depreciation
- Repair estimate
- Surveyor assessment
- Applicable policy limits

7. MANUAL REVIEW

A claim should be referred for manual review when:

- Important information is missing.
- The incident description is unclear.
- Documents conflict with each other.
- Fraud is suspected.
- The claimed amount is unusually high.
- Policy coverage cannot be determined confidently.
