PRIVATE MOTOR POLICY EXCLUSIONS

The insurer will not pay for loss or damage caused by or arising from:

1. NORMAL WEAR AND TEAR

Gradual deterioration, ageing, corrosion, rust, and ordinary wear are not covered.

2. MECHANICAL OR ELECTRICAL BREAKDOWN

Mechanical or electrical failure is not covered unless it directly results from a covered insured event or an applicable endorsement.

3. DRIVING UNDER THE INFLUENCE

Claims may be denied when the driver was under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or another intoxicating substance.

4. INVALID DRIVING LICENCE

Claims may be denied when the driver did not possess a valid and appropriate driving licence.

5. UNAUTHORIZED USE

The policy does not cover loss arising while the vehicle is used outside the permitted policy purpose.

6. RACING OR SPEED TESTING

Loss arising from racing, speed trials, or competitive events is excluded.

7. INTENTIONAL DAMAGE

Deliberate or intentional damage caused by the insured or driver is excluded.

8. CONSEQUENTIAL LOSS

Indirect losses or consequential losses are excluded unless specifically covered through an endorsement.

9. FRAUD OR MISREPRESENTATION

A claim may be denied when false, misleading, or fraudulent information is provided.

10. WAR AND NUCLEAR RISKS

Loss caused by war, invasion, nuclear risk, or radioactive contamination is excluded.
